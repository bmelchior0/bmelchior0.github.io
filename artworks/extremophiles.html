Collective Metabolism of Extremophiles

Joel Rännäri and Berenike Melchior, 2024

Live bacteria culture, blown glass sculptures,
recirculating water system, polyurethane foam stand,
50 × 190 cm

“Collective Metabolism of Extremophiles” is a collaborative bioartwork by Joel Rännäri and Berenike Melchior. It simulates a future in which severely eutrophicated, near-boiling seas allow only extremophiles to survive. The artwork was designed to function as an ideal environment for bacterial growth - an ambition perhaps fulfilled too successfully. A mixed culture of rogue bacteria ultimately overran and annihilated the spirulina culture that was initially introduced into the system, demonstrating unpredictable ecological dynamics.
