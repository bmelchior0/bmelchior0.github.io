"A hidden eye that sees? Not much." is a videographic documentation of a dance performance captured on a webcam on October 15, 2023. A dancer enters public space, moves, creating micro disruptions in the uniform motion of Sunday afternoon strolls. A camera is fastened somewhere above, continuously streaming images into the public space of the world wide web.
